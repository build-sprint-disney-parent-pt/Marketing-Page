Disney Parent

Welcome to the Disney Parent Page, where you can connect with other parents, swap strollers and child care passes. Other parents can watch your children while you enjoy the rides and vice versa.

Benefits of using Disney Parent

Sit back and relax, the page connects you other parents with similar interests: having FUN!

How to use this page

It's very easy. Simply sign up with the login button above. There, you can post questions, post your location. Other Disney Parents can meet you there and you can swap children or parents

Cost

There is no additional cost for using the site. It is included with cost of your ticket.
